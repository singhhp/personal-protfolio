## Overview

The **Logistics Performance Dashboard** was designed for **Global Freight Solutions** to provide real-time visibility into shipment operations, delivery performance, and service-level efficiency. It enables logistics managers to quickly identify patterns in delayed shipments, monitor exception trends, and compare performance across domestic and international routes.

This dashboard centralizes logistics KPIs, offering a single platform to track key operational metrics and optimize transport performance across multiple shipping zones and services.

---

## Key Features

- **Comprehensive Shipment KPIs**: Displays metrics such as total shipments, average transit time, on-time vs delayed shipments, domestic vs international percentages, and shipments with exceptions.  
- **Trend Visualization**: Tracks **average transit time by month**, helping identify seasonal fluctuations and service bottlenecks.  
- **Zone-Level Analysis**: Highlights shipment performance, delays, and exception rates across all operational zones.  
- **Service Type Insights**: Compares **total shipments and delay percentages** across service types (e.g., International Priority, FedEx Express Saver).  
- **Dynamic Filters**: Users can filter by **Month** and **Year** to explore shipment performance for specific time frames.

---

## Technologies Used

- **Power BI**: For visualization, data modeling, and interactivity.  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**: For calculating KPIs like delay percentages, transit times, and zone-level performance.  
- **Excel & CSV Data Sources**: Containing shipment logs, service-level details, and delivery outcomes.  
- **Power Query**: For data transformation, cleaning, and combining multiple logistics datasets.  

---

## Challenges and Learnings

The primary challenge was designing DAX measures that accurately categorized shipments as **on-time, delayed, or with exceptions** across multiple zones and service types. Additionally, maintaining performance while handling 20,000+ records required careful data model optimization and the use of calculated columns only when necessary.  

This project enhanced understanding of **data modeling for operational analytics**, **filter context management**, and **KPI visualization techniques** in Power BI.

---

## Outcome

The Logistics Performance Dashboard provided Global Freight Solutions with an **end-to-end operational view**, improving **delivery reliability tracking** and **service performance analysis**.  
By automating manual reporting, the team reduced weekly reporting time by **over 70%** and gained the ability to pinpoint recurring delay patterns across service zones.  

This dashboard became a vital decision-making tool for improving delivery SLAs and optimizing route efficiency across the logistics network.
