## Overview

The **Dynamic Revenue Performance Dashboard** was developed to empower Haven Hotels Group’s Revenue Management team with real-time insights into property performance across different brands and cities. The dashboard consolidates bookings, revenue, and occupancy data to highlight growth trends and identify high-performing hotels based on key metrics such as ADR, RevPAR, and occupancy rates.  

This project provides executives with a single source of truth for monitoring revenue health, optimizing pricing strategies, and improving overall room utilization.

---

## Key Features

- **Interactive Filters**: Users can slice data by **Brand**, **City**, **Year**, and **Month**, enabling granular performance analysis across locations.  
- **Comprehensive KPIs**: Displays metrics including total bookings, completed bookings, gross revenue, room revenue, ADR, RevPAR, occupancy rate, and growth percentages.  
- **Growth Trend Analysis**: Automatically calculates **Month-over-Month (MoM)** and **Year-over-Year (YoY)** performance for revenue metrics using DAX formulas.  
- **Property Ranking**: Dynamically ranks Haven Hotels properties by RevPAR, helping leadership identify top and underperforming hotels.  
- **Visual Storytelling**: Combines bar charts, line graphs, and KPI cards for intuitive insights into monthly revenue patterns and booking activity.

---

## Technologies Used

- **Power BI**: Core platform for data modeling, visualization, and dashboard interactivity.  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**: Used for advanced calculations like ADR, RevPAR, MoM, and YoY growth.  
- **Excel & SQL Data Sources**: Source data for bookings, revenue, and room availability.  
- **Power Query**: For data transformation and ETL (extract, transform, load) operations.  

---

## Challenges and Learnings

One of the main challenges was creating **accurate time-based growth comparisons** while handling missing data for early months. Implementing DAX measures with correct context transition and filtering logic was crucial for ensuring valid MoM and YoY results. Additionally, optimizing the dashboard for performance involved reducing query loads and improving model efficiency.  

This project deepened understanding of **DAX performance tuning**, **measure dependencies**, and **data modeling best practices** in Power BI.

---

## Outcome

The Dynamic Revenue Performance Dashboard streamlined Haven Hotels Group’s monthly reporting process, **reducing manual reporting time by over 60%**. Executives now have a clear, interactive overview of brand-level performance, leading to **data-driven pricing decisions** and improved revenue management strategies.  

The dashboard serves as a foundation for future enhancements such as forecasting models and automated alerting on key performance deviations.
